Ask forgiveness not permission

At the start of last year the British Council pitched to IELTS - its International English Language Testing partners, an ambition to develop the first product that fully aligned language learning with testing.

With the vast majority of it's global teaching and testing centres closed, the organisation felt it had an opportunity to develop an offer that benefitted the increasing number of digital natives with accessible enabling technology.

The decision was made to invite Senior Leadership to engage in a conversation about the opportunity, for which a significant number of discussion aids needed to be prepared.
