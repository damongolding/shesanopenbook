February 24, 2020

And although there wasn't a budget (or time) for set up focus groups I encouraged Mel to share the work with as many of her course attendees, her mentors, colleagues, friends and family.

It’s always enlightening to see your designs being used and abused, and you’re guaranteed to learn lots about your potential customers, your product and your client.

This valuable insight both corrected misconceptions and validated assumptions. It helped Mel's business (especially as a start-up) to be more user-centred and made sure I, as the designed, remained open-minded to feedback.
