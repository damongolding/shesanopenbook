Who is she?

I am a daughter, a wife and a mother. 'She' is the moniker I've had for twenty-something years. I am quite genuinely and unapologetically an open book.

I'm a trained Information Designer and an experienced team leader. I run engaging creative workshops. I love project planning and strategy and spend a lot of my time defining and prioritising briefs.

I'm also a conscientious problem-solver. I'm extremely organised. I listen, I ask challenging questions and I work really, really hard.

Knowledge is empathy

I aspire to embed inclusive design principles throughout my work. I design in the medium that will communicate a concept most effectively, adapting the fidelity to gain insight more efficiently.

I advocate, participate and facilitate in usability testing.

There are a range of projects on this site that demonstrate my approach, but the content is not exhaustive. If you would like to know more please send me a message.


She's easily read. Designer, circle-lover, daughter, wife and mother.
