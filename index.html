Who is she?

I am a daughter, wife and mother. 'She' is the moniker I've had for almost twenty years. I am quite genuinely an open book and unapologetically easy to read.

I'm a trained Visual Designer and an experienced team leader. I run engaging creative workshops, love project planning and strategy and spend a lot of my time defining and prioritising briefs.

I'm extremely organised and a conscientious and committed problem-solver, both personally and professionally. I listen, I ask challenging questions and I work really, really hard.

You'll find lots of examples of my projects and design work on this site. And if you want me to talk to me about any of it, or you just fancy a chat you can contact me via this web form.

She's easily read. Visual Designer, circle-lover, daughter, wife and mother.
