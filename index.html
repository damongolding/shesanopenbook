Who is she?

I am a daughter, a wife and a mother. 'She' is the moniker I've had for almost twenty years. I am quite genuinely and unapologetically an open book.

I'm a trained Visual Designer and an experienced team leader. I run engaging creative workshops, I love project planning and strategy and spend a lot of my time defining and prioritising briefs.

I'm also extremely organised and a conscientious and committed problem-solver. I listen, I ask challenging questions and I work really, really hard.

Stubborn about goals, flexible about methods

You'll find a range of my projects and designs on this site. What you won't see is how I respond to a brief.

I will adapt my methods to suit my audience and to ensure the right message is being communicated effectively. The decision on what fidelity and format I design in is led by what will gain the most insight efficiently with users.

As a user-centred designer I advocate inclusivity and aspire to embed good accessibility practices throughout my team and our work.

If you want to know more you can contact me via a web form.

She's easily read. Visual Designer, circle-lover, daughter, wife and mother.
