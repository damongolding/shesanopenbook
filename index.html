Needs a title

8 February 2022

My name is Jo. 'Easily read' is the moniker I've had for twenty-something years. I am quite genuinely and unapologetically an open book.

I'm a trained Information Designer and experienced Team Leader. I run engaging creative workshops. I love project planning and strategy and spend a lot of my time defining and prioritising briefs.

I'm also a conscientious problem-solver. I'm extremely organised. I listen, I ask challenging questions and I work really, really hard.

Empathy is knowledge

16 January 2022

I aspire to embed inclusive design principles throughout my team and our work. I'm an advocate for all forms of usability testing. I design in a medium that will communicate a concept most effectively and at the fidelity most appropriate for the audience.

There are a range of projects on this site that demonstrate my approach, but the content is not exhaustive. If you would like to know more please get in touch.
