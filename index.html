Who is she?

I am a daughter, a wife and a mother. 'She' is the moniker I've had for almost twenty years. I am quite genuinely and unapologetically an open book.

I'm a trained Information Designer and an experienced Team Leader. I run engaging creative workshops, I love project planning and strategy and spend a lot of my time defining and prioritising briefs.

I'm also extremely organised and a conscientious and committed problem-solver. I listen, I ask challenging questions and I work really, really hard.

Knowledge is empathy

As a user-centred designer I advocate inclusivity and aspire to embed good accessibility practices throughout my team and our work.

The decision on what medium or fidelity I design in is led by what will communicate an idea most efficiently. I adapt my methods to gain insight most effectively from my audience.

There are a range of projects on this site that will help demonstrate how I respond to a brief, but the content isn't exhaustive.

If you want to know more, or you just want to get to know me, you can contact me via a web form.

She's easily read. Visual Designer, circle-lover, daughter, wife and mother.
